What This Unlocks — Per-Method Experience

If method = Online / Zelle / Venmo
Contribute page pre-highlights their preferred method. Charity Zelle/account details already visible. Member taps, pays, done.
If method = DAF (Fidelity, Schwab, etc.)
DAF Direct widget opens their sponsor by default. Charity EIN pre-filled. Grant recommendation pre-populated. Member confirms and submits.
If method = QCD (age 70½+)
QCD path appears prominently. Instruction letter pre-filled with their custodian's name and charity's address. Download and email to custodian takes 2 minutes.
If method = Check
Check handling instructions shown. IRA check slip (for QCD via checkbook) pre-filled with charity info. No personalization needed from profile.
Members who skip the profile still get the full Contribute page — they just choose from all options each time.
