Welcome to the 100 Men Who Care Wood River Valley document library. This site contains all governing documents, operational guides, and strategic proposals for our giving circle. Founded in 2013 by Marty Lyon, we've contributed over $757,000 to local charities through collective generosity.

Wireframes & Journey Maps

Member Customer Journey Map

Full lifecycle from awareness through long-term membership. Seven-stage journey map covering actions, thoughts, touchpoints, and what information the system needs to collect from each member to enable electronic donations. Includes deep-dive on the payment profile setup screen.

WIREFRAME

Member Donate Workflow

Mobile-first storyboard of the complete donation flow: entry points (QR at meeting, post-meeting email), the Contribute page with three-charity allocation, payment method selection, six method-completion screens (Online, Zelle, DAF, QCD, Check, Venmo/PayPal), the "I've Donated" confirmation form, and the Coordinator's participation tracker.

WIREFRAME

Coordinator Workflow

Seven-phase quarterly cycle storyboard for the Communications Coordinator: nomination review and finalist selection, charity electronic payment setup (the critical info-collection step), announcement email, meeting day tools, post-meeting email, participation tracking dashboard, matching list export, and impact reporting.

WIREFRAME

Beneficiary (Charity) Workflow

End-to-end storyboard for the recipient organization: selection notification, payment info form (EIN, Zelle, ACH, Fidelity enrollment), pre-meeting prep checklist, what the incoming gift stream looks like, receiving and using the matching list to identify DAF/QCD donors, acknowledgment letter template, and impact report back to 100MWC.
