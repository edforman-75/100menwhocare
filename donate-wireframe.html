Complete Flow Summary

Entry
At-meeting QR scan or Post-meeting email button/QR (Entry B also triggers 2-week follow-up if no confirmation logged)
Screen 2
Contribute Page — member sees all 3 charities, enters amounts totaling $100+ (default: full $100 to first charity)
Screen 3
Payment Method Selection — repeated once per charity that received a gift; fee-free methods listed first
Screen 4
Method completion: 4A Online 4B Zelle 4C DAF 4D QCD 4E Check 4F Venmo/PayPal
Screen 5
"I've Donated" form — confirms methods per charity; DAF/QCD fields expand to capture sponsor name for matching list
Screen 6
Thank-you confirmation with per-charity summary and tax receipt guidance
Back-end
Coordinator dashboard shows live participation; exports matching list to each charity for DAF/QCD attribution
Design constraints honored: No money passes through the Group  ·  All three charities receive equal visual treatment  ·  Fee-free methods prominent  ·  Honor system throughout  ·  Works for check writers, Zelle senders, DAF donors, and QCD retirees  ·  Coordinator workload: ~30 min/quarter
